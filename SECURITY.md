## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a potential security vulnerability in this project, please report it immediately to the maintainers.

**Please do not open a public issue.** Instead, send an email to [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS].

When reporting a vulnerability, please include:
- A description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce it.
- Possible impact.

We respond to all reports as quickly as possible and will keep you updated on the progress of a fix.
